Perception of Artistic Style

Kevin Zuern

Queen's University

Overview

In this presentation we will talk about current research in artistic style transfer. We will look at a novel approach to representing artistic style in a neural network as well as some further developments that have been since been published.

We will be focusing on the Gatys et. al. paper "A Neural Algorithm of Artistic Style". Their novel technique allows one to apply the artistic style of a reference image to an arbitrary source image.

Examples

Terminology

Content

The subject of an image. In the example the content is a night sky and a town in a valley.

Style

The result of an artist's subjective interpretation of a scene.

How Does it Work?

They use a convolutional neural network (ANN built to mimic structure & properties of visual cortex in brain) as the basis for the algorithm.

Each layer in the CNN consists of a collection of neurons acting like image filters. Each neuron extracts a certain feature from the image. The higher level layers care less about the pixel values and more about the content of the image.
